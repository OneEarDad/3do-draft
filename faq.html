Privacy & Compliance
Patient information is de-identified before anything is transmitted for fabrication: HIPAA-aligned and built for VA and DOD environments.
The scan file we receive doesn’t contain identifying information. The patient’s identity stays inside your facility; what we see is the foot, and the prescription and accommodations the provider chooses.
Yes. AccuScan DP was specifically designed to meet the compliance requirements of federal healthcare environments. We are FDA Registered (RN #3015146490), SAM registered (CAGE: 9PXW1), and hold SDVOSB and WOSB designations. AccuScan DP was also recognized as Most Improved Product by the VA Podiatry Nationally, validating its effectiveness in VA settings.
Training & Support
Yes. We provide on-site training and support to help podiatrists and staff in veteran hospitals become fully comfortable with our AccuScan DP system and workflow. Our goal is to ensure every member of your team can achieve optimal scanning results for their patients. Support doesn't end at setup. We remain available as an ongoing partner throughout your use of the system.
De-identified digital scanning built for federal facilities, a veteran-focused team (SDVOSB), and hands-on partnership with your clinicians, not a faceless remote lab.
Ordering & Logistics
Reach us directly by phone at (844) FOOT-911 or by email at Orders@3DOrthotics.com. We work with each facility individually to build a plan that fits your specific patient volume, specialty needs, and logistical requirements. There's no one-size-fits-all approach. Every hospital engagement is custom.
Yes. Free shipping is included on all completed orthotic orders, shipped directly to your facility. There are no hidden logistics costs.
The AccuScan DP system is designed to complete a full patient foot scan in five minutes or less, from the patient sitting down to a completed scan file ready for transmission. Compared to traditional cast molding, this dramatically reduces chair time and eliminates the mess and curing wait associated with plaster or foam box methods.
