Our Story

Built by Veterans.
Built for Veterans.

3D Orthotics was founded in 2023 by a team with over 50 years of combined experience in durable medical equipment, surgical implants, and medical device distribution. All united by a mission to modernize orthotic care for those who served.

Origin

A Problem Worth Solving

Traditional cast molding, while accurate, remained messy and time-consuming. Digital scanning emerged as the obvious upgrade: faster, cleaner, and more precise. But federal healthcare systems faced a hard barrier. HIPAA regulations prevented the digital transmission of Protected Health Information (PHI).

That gap left VA and DOD hospitals stuck with outdated methods. Our team, with decades of federal healthcare experience, identified the problem, engineered the solution, and built AccuScan DP: the only fully HIPAA-compliant digital orthotic scanning program in existence.

We don't just supply products. We collaborate with podiatrists and staff to ensure every veteran gets the orthotic care they deserve.
