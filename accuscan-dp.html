The Problem We Solved

Digital Scanning.
Federal Compliance.

VA and DOD hospitals knew digital scanning was superior to cast molding: faster, cleaner, more accurate. But transmitting 3D scan data meant transmitting Protected Health Information (PHI), and that conflicted directly with HIPAA regulations.

AccuScan DP de-identifies patient information before scan data leaves your facility. Digital speed and accuracy, HIPAA-aligned, built for federal care.
