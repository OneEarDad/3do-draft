The Problem We Solved

Digital Scanning.
Federal Compliance.

VA and DOD hospitals knew digital scanning was superior to cast molding: faster, cleaner, more accurate. But transmitting 3D scan data meant transmitting Protected Health Information (PHI), and that conflicted directly with HIPAA regulations.

AccuScan DP is our answer: a complete workflow that strips all identifying patient data before any file leaves the facility. The result is a 100% digital process with zero PHI exposure. The only system of its kind approved for federal healthcare environments.
